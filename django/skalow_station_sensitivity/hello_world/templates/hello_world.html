SKA-Low sensitivity calculator (version 1.00)

This service allows users to calculate SKA-Low station sensitivity (A/T [m2/K]) and expected imaging noise using EDA2 and AAVS2 prototype station simulations, as described in Sokolowski et al. PASA, 2022.

Select from the following options (where LST is Local Sidereal Time):

Imaging sensitivity of the SKA-Low telescope and sensitivity A/T [m2/K] of a single station as a function of LST (or Hour Angle) [hours] for a source at a specified RA and DEC [deg] position and a range of Hour Angle [hours]
The assumptions in regards to calculation of the imaging noise (natural weighting etc.) will be listed on the output page.

Select from the following options to calculate sensitivity of an individual SKA-Low station:

    • At a specified frequency and pointing direction in Azimuth and Elevation [deg] as a function of LST:
    • At a specified frequency and pointing direction in RA and DEC [deg] as a function of LST:
