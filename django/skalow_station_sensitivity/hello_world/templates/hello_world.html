SKA-Low station sensitivity

This service allows to calculate SKA-Low station sensitivity (A/T [m^2/K]) using EDA2 and AAVS2 prototype station simulations.
Please, select from the following options:
  • Sensitivity vs. frequency at a given pointing direction and LST
  • All-sky sensitivity map for a given frequency and LST
  • Sensitivity A/T [m^2/K] vs. lst
