SKA-Low sensitivity calculator

This service allows users to calculate SKA-Low station sensitivity (A/T [m2/K]) and expected imaging noise using EDA2 and AAVS2 prototype station simulations, as described in Sokolowski et al. PASA, 2022
The assumptions in regards to calculation of the imaging noise (natural weighting etc.) are listed on the page implementing this specific option. Select from the following options (LST is Local Sidereal Time):
  • Imaging sensitivity and A/T [m2/K] vs. LST or Hour Angle [hours] for a source at a specified RA and DEC [deg] position and a range of Hour Angle [hours]
    • Imaging sensitivity for a source at a specified RA and DEC [deg] and Hour Angle [hours] range
